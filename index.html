NATHAN HON

About Me

Nathan Hon is a future software engineer, an experienced leader, and a fan of Football Club Bayern Munich. He is currently completing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Southern Methodist University.

Before continuing his education at SMU, Nathan served as a Lieutenant in the United States Army within the Field Artillery branch. He was stationed in Fort Drum, New York as well as in Camp Casey, South Korea where he met his wife. The common thread through all of his experiences has been combining servant leadership, an active learning mindset, and technical experience to solve problems with innovative solutions.

He is expecting to graduate with a B.A. in Computer Science from Southern Methodist University and also holds a B.A. in International Studies from Texas A&M University.
